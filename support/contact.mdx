## Response Times

- **Free tier**: 48-72 hours
- **Pro tier**: 24-48 hours

## Before Contacting Support

1. Check the [FAQ](/support/faq) for common solutions
2. Review the [Quickstart Guide](/quickstart)
3. Check [status.influship.com](https://status.influship.com) for service updates

## Information to Include

When contacting support, please provide:

- Your account email
- API key (masked: `sk_...1234`)
- Error messages and codes
- Steps to reproduce the issue
- Request/response examples
